IEA 15MW Monopile Design

Ocean Renewable Energy Course (UMass MIE 697CE)
Spring 2020

A report detailing the sizing and design of a monopile support structure for the International Energy Agency (IEA) 15 MW offshore wind reference turbine. Here, I developed a full-scale, finite element structural analysis ANSYS model (stress, deformation, modal, and buckling analyses) which incorporates hydrodynamic loads computed in ANSYS Aqwa, rotor and tower aerodynamic loads, gravitational loads, and monopile embedment into a nonlinear soil continuum. This model was used to perform a parametric optimization on monopile diameter and thickness.
