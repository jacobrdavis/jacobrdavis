Capstone Senior Design Project

Compost-based greenhouse heating system. As a proof-of-concept, our team developed a scaled helical coil heat exchanger designed to extract heat from the exothermic composting process. In practice, this heat is transferred to a water or glycol-based pipe system which is fed beneath the greenhouse beds to provide an additional source of heat. Unfortunately, we didn't have the time (or money) to build a full scale heat exchanger to install in the actual greeenhouse (shown left).
