About Me


At UMass, I am mechanical engineering master's student and a member of Professor Krish Sharman's Ocean Resources and Renewable Energy Laboratory (ORRE). Our work focuses on the hydrodynamics of offshore wind, marine and hydrokinetic renewable energy technologies, moorings, and aquaculture systems. My current thesis work, a collaborative project with the National Renewable Energy Laboratory, pertains to the development of a wave energy converter which employs variable geometry modules to control and shed loads during operation in extreme seas.
